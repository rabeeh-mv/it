KOOL KITE's Open Online Learning

KOOL

As part of Public Education Rejuvenation Mission, all class rooms in secondary and higher secondary sections in the State of Kerala has been converted as Hi-Tech class rooms and IT/ICT enabled education has become a reality. But deputing teachers for different training programmes in working days adversely affects the academic days and academic activities of students. Now-a-days Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) are very popular and is much effective. In line with this – KITE has developed a special tool for online courses/training; KITE's OPEN ONLINE LEARNING – KOOL., an online general tool to impart training/ courses to teachers, students, parents and public.
