Haritha Vidyalayam 2022

Reality show prepared by the Department of Public Education

Kerala has gained matchless achievements in the field of education enriching the schools to the international level infrastructures. Teachers' efforts behind this honor are worth saying. There are sincere endeavours from other people and institutions including educational workers non-teaching staff, technical workers etc. Haritha Vidyalayam Season 2 is a reality show prepared by the Department of Public Education with the aim of presenting such achievements and those behind such achievements in an attractive manner.
